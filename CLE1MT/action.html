Physical Distancing

Keep a distance of at least 1m from each other

Wear a mask

A well-fitting mask keeps the virus from reaching others. It can also protect the wearer from becoming infected.

Sneezing Etiquette

Covering coughs and sneezes and keeping hands clean can help prevent the spread of the Covid-19

Wash your hands

Proper handwashing washes microbes including Covid-19 diseases causing germs from skin

Get a Vaccination against Covid-19

Vaccines work by training and preparing the body’s natural defences – the immune system – to recognize and fight off the viruses and bacteria they target.
